Enhancing Crop Disease Prediction Using Deep Learning

Enhancing Crop Disease Prediction Using Deep Learning is an advanced AI-powered application designed to help farmers, gardeners, and agricultural professionals identify plant diseases quickly and accurately. By leveraging deep learning technology, our system can detect various crop diseases from a simple image of a plant leaf.

Features
Real-time Disease Detection

Instantly analyze plant leaves for signs of disease

Works with various crops including Apple, Tomato, Potato, and more

Detailed Information

Get comprehensive disease information and treatment recommendations

Cross-Platform Compatibility

Works on both desktop and mobile devices

How It Works
Capture

Take or upload a photo of the plant leaf

Process

Our AI model analyzes the image

Detect

Identifies diseases with bounding boxes

Report

Provides detailed analysis and recommendations

Technology

Our application uses YOLOv8, a state-of-the-art object detection model, to identify and localize plant diseases. The model has been trained on a diverse dataset of plant leaves with various diseases, enabling it to accurately detect and classify multiple crop diseases.

The system processes images at a resolution of 480x480 pixels, optimizing for both accuracy and performance across different devices. For images of other sizes, the application automatically resizes them while preserving the aspect ratio.
